FAQ's

How does the mobile wash work?

Book your service, share where your car is parked, and our worker will come to that location to clean your vehicle on-site.

Do I need to provide water?

No. The car wash professional will bring water and the necessary equipment to complete the job.

Where can my car be cleaned?

Your car can be cleaned at home, at work, or at another approved location where the vehicle is parked and accessible.

Do I need to be there the whole time?

You only need to make sure the vehicle can be accessed. Once the wash is complete, we pack up and leave your space tidy.

What should I remove from the car?

Please remove valuables and personal items you do not want moved before the appointment begins.

What do you bring with you?

We bring water, cleaning products, towels, and the other tools needed to give your car a professional mobile wash.
